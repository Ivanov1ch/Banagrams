Features

Flexible Lobbies

Control the size and visibility of your lobby to play with strangers or with friends. Give yourself and your lobbies names for easy identification.

Drag and Drop System

Drag and drop your tiles into a large, scrollable grid system. Build your crosswords quickly without ever having to worry about running out of space.

Full Bunch Usage

Just like in a physical game of Bananagrams, every piece in the bunch will be used, without repeats, until no more tiles may be peeled. Say goodbye to the lucky and unlucky bunches that plague Bananagrams over the phone.
